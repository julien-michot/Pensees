# Protocol for an induced OBE|Receptions

According to few sources, some people can trigger an OBE by repeating to oneself: "I want to do an out of body experience tonight" several times.

I'm currently experimenting by forcing a natural wake-up at 5am, then telling myself that I'm ready for OBE or reception and 'sleep' again while trying to keep to consciousness. 

The easiest way for me to naturally wake up at 5am and still be able to sleep is to take a nap after lunch (good thing I'm in Vietnam!).



## Results

### Receptions
So far, a few (2) downloads happened where a clear and profound feeling that a specific static image has to be remembered at wake-up. Parts of the images were visually clear, with edges, colors and semantics, the rest was unreachable by vision but very precise on the semantics: which concept, how many instances and where. It's quite a disturbing feeling to try to see something at that stage, something that you deeply know what it is only to fail to observe colors and just 'see' emptiness.

### OBE
So far, no OBE, not even early signs (e.g. vibrations).
